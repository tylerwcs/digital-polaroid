# How to use the Digital Wall

This app lets guests share photos and short notes on a big screen—like a live photo wall with Polaroid-style cards.

---

## For guests (phones)

**Open the upload page**  
Use the link or QR code shown on the display. You’ll see a camera-style screen with the title “Share the Moments!”

**Add a photo**  
Tap the large lens (it may say “TAP TO SNAP”). Your device will ask you to take a new picture or choose one from your gallery.

**Or send text only**  
Tap **Write a Note** if you prefer just a message, without a photo.

**Write your message**  
After you add a photo (or start a text-only note), a Polaroid-style card appears. Type your note in the space under the picture. There is a short length limit so everything stays readable on the wall.

**Optional: draw on your photo**  
If you uploaded a picture, you can tap **Add Signature to Photo**, then draw with your finger. Pick black, white, or gold, and use **Clear Signature** if you want to start over.

**Post it**  
Tap **Post to Wall**. You should see a confirmation that it was posted. If something isn’t allowed (for example, the note doesn’t pass the automatic check), the app will tell you and you can try different wording.

**Change your mind**  
**Cancel** clears what you’re working on and takes you back to the start.

---

## For the big screen (the wall)

**Main wall**  
The display page shows a flowing grid of everyone’s Polaroids over a background video. In the corner you’ll usually see a **Scan to Upload** QR code so guests can join without typing a URL.

**When someone posts**  
New posts often appear large in the center for a few seconds (a “spotlight”), then settle into the scrolling wall with the rest.

**Alternate layout**  
If your host opens the **six-column** version of the wall, you’ll see more columns of cards and typically no big center spotlight—new items simply appear in the grid.

**Empty wall**  
If nothing has been shared yet, you may see a “waiting” message until the first post arrives.

---

## For organizers (admin)

**Open the admin page**  
Your host will give you the admin web address. Sign in with the **password they provide** (do not share it publicly).

**Review submissions**  
You’ll see a dashboard with every photo and note, newest first.

**Remove something**  
Move your pointer over a card and choose **Delete** if a post should come down. You’ll be asked to confirm.

**Save everything**  
**Download All Photos** prepares a ZIP file of the submissions so you can keep copies after the event.

---

## Tips

- **Wi‑Fi**: Guests and the display should be on a reliable connection so posts show up right away.
- **Camera permission**: The first time someone taps to add a photo, the browser may ask to use the camera or photos—choosing “allow” makes sharing easier.
- **QR code**: Scanning the code on the wall is usually the fastest way for guests to get to the upload page.

If something won’t post, check the message on screen, try again, or ask the person running the event for help.
